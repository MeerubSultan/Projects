My Autobiography

Meerub Sultan

My name is Meerub Sultan and I am the creator behind the new Cougar Cafe. I have been working in the culinary industry for well over 30 years now, and was given the opportunity to create something great here for the University of Houston at Sugarland. At the Cafe you will find a wide selection of carefully selected choices for the students of UH Sugarland. Using my experince I have created a blend of restaurants and cuisines that I believe you all will enjoy. Although I take great pride in what I have created here, I understand that there is always room for improvement. If you have any suggestions in making the Cougar Cafe a better place, please feel free to contact me at this email.
